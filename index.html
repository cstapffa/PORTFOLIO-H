About Me

Digital Designer based in Uruguay

Hi, I’m Camila Stapff (but you can call me Mila). I’m a digital designer who believes that design is more than just making things look good. For me, it’s about connecting people, crafting experiences that feel intuitive, and communicating ideas with honesty and detail.

My path into design wasn’t linear, it grew out of curiosity, a love for visual expression, and a desire to bring order to creative chaos. I come from a tech-rooted family but found my place where code meets color, structure meets play, and emotion meets function.

I care about coherence, I enjoy the tension between utility and ornament, and I believe that every project carries the chance to say something, or make someone feel something.

This space is a reflection of who I am, what I do, and how I think. Hope you find something that speaks to you.
