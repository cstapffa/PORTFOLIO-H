Hi, I’m Camila Stapff (but you can call me Mila). I’m a passionate and creative digital designer based in Uruguay. With a background in web design, graphic design, and a deep love for photography, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and artistic flair to everything I create.

My journey into design started at a young age, where I spent countless hours experimenting with analog photography and exploring different artistic styles. Over the years, I’ve developed a strong appreciation for blending traditional techniques with modern, digital design, especially through the power of color, shapes, and dynamic compositions.

I specialize in creating visually engaging, user-centered designs for websites, brands, and digital experiences. Whether it’s a clean and simple website layout, a striking logo, or a cohesive brand identity, I aim to create designs that are not only beautiful but also functional and impactful.

I believe design is about more than just making things look good—it’s about telling a story and connecting with people on a deeper level. And that’s what I strive to do with every project I take on. If you’re looking for a designer who brings creativity, passion, and a tech-savvy approach, I’d love to collaborate with you. Let’s bring your ideas to life!
